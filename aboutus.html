About Us

Welcome to Digital Signify! Our application is designed to help you create, save, and manage digital signatures easily. Below are the steps on how to use the application:

  • To draw your signature, use the canvas on the main page. You can customize the pen style, color, and size using the controls on the left.
  • Once you are satisfied with your signature, you can clear the canvas or save the signature as a PNG file using the buttons on the right.
  • To scan a JPG image of a document, navigate to the "Scan Image" page using the link in the navbar.
  • Upload your JPG file and our tool will convert it into a scanned image for easy digital handling.
